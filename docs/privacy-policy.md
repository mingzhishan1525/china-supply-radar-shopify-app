# Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

## Introduction
China Supply Radar ("we", "us", "our") provides a Shopify app that helps store owners manage inventory, plan for Chinese holidays, and optimize reorder timing. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

## Data We Collect
When you install and use China Supply Radar, we collect the following data from your Shopify store:

1. **Shopify Store Data**:
   - Shop domain, access token, and installation status
   - Product information (SKUs, titles, inventory levels, variants)
   - Order history (order dates, line items, quantities, fulfillment status)
   - Inventory levels and locations

2. **Supplier Mapping Data**:
   - Any supplier information you manually enter or map to your products
   - Lead time and reorder threshold settings you configure

3. **Usage Data**:
   - App feature usage statistics to improve our service
   - Error logs for debugging purposes

## Important Note: Customer Personal Data
**We do not collect or store any personal customer data**, including but not limited to:
- Customer names, emails, phone numbers, or addresses
- Payment information or billing details
- Any other personally identifiable information about your customers

## How We Use Your Data
We use the collected data for the following purposes:
1. To provide our core services: inventory calculation, reorder recommendations, holiday risk alerts
2. To sync product and order data from your Shopify store
3. To improve our app features and user experience
4. To troubleshoot technical issues and provide support
5. To communicate important app updates or service changes

## Data Retention
- Shop session and access token data is retained for as long as you have the app installed
- When you uninstall the app, we mark your session as uninstalled and retain anonymized aggregate data
- If you request full deletion of your shop data via the Shopify SHOP_REDACT webhook, all your shop-specific data is permanently deleted within 7 days
- Anonymized aggregate usage statistics (that cannot be linked to your specific shop) may be retained indefinitely for product improvement

## Data Deletion
You can request full deletion of your shop data at any time by:
1. Uninstalling the app from your Shopify store
2. Sending a deletion request to support@chinasupplyradar.com with your shop domain
3. Using the Shopify GDPR shop deletion tool, which triggers our SHOP_REDACT webhook

## Data Sharing
We do not share, sell, or rent your data to any third parties, except:
- When required by law or legal process
- To protect our rights, property, or safety
- With your explicit consent

## Security
We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, use, or disclosure:
- All access tokens are encrypted at rest
- All data transfers use HTTPS encryption
- Access to production data is restricted to authorized personnel only

## GDPR / CCPA Rights
If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA) or California, you have the following rights:
1. Right to access the personal data we hold about you (note: we do not hold personal customer data)
2. Right to request correction or deletion of your data
3. Right to restrict processing of your data
4. Right to data portability
5. Right to withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@chinasupplyradar.com.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on our website and updating the "Last updated" date.
