## Basic Information

### App Name

China Supply Radar

### Short Description

Avoid stockouts from Chinese factory holidays with reorder timing insights.

### Full Description

China Supply Radar helps Shopify merchants plan inventory purchases around Chinese New Year, Golden Week, and other factory holiday slowdowns.

The app syncs product inventory and order history, calculates sales velocity, lets merchants map products to suppliers, and highlights reorder timing risk before major China factory holidays.

Core benefits:

- Prevent holiday stockout surprises with advance factory closure risk visibility.
- Estimate inventory cover days from real Shopify order history.
- Track supplier lead times for Chinese manufacturers.
- Prioritize products that need reorder attention before holidays.
- Replace spreadsheet-only reorder planning with a Shopify Admin workflow.

Key features:

- Product and inventory sync from Shopify.
- Order history sync for sales velocity calculations.
- Supplier lead time management.
- Inventory cover days calculation.
- Reorder timing recommendations.
- China holiday risk calendar.
- Reorder queue prioritization.

Best for merchants who source products from Chinese suppliers and want a practical inventory planning workflow before major factory holiday periods.

## App Store FAQ

### What data does the app use?

The app uses product information, inventory quantities, supplier mappings entered by the merchant, and order line item history needed to calculate sales velocity and reorder timing.

### Does the app store customer personal information?

No. The app does not need or store customer names, emails, phone numbers, addresses, payment data, or other customer PII.

### Why does the app request `read_orders`?

The app uses order line item quantities and variant IDs to estimate sales velocity. Sales velocity is needed to calculate inventory cover days and reorder timing recommendations.

### Is billing enabled?

No. The current launch path is Free Beta. No Shopify charges are made at this time.
